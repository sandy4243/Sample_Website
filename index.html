Integrations

CRM systems

CRM system is home for your sales team and Clarizen is home for your project teams. To unlock maximum efficiency between your sales team and your delivery team an integration between the 2 systems is typically needed. We have supported integrations for multiple CRMs, listed below and we can support custom integrations for any CRMs not listed below.

    Use cases supported:
  • Opportunity to Project mapping: Once your sales team marks an opportunity as “won”, our CRM integration will automatically fire up and create a project for the won opportunity in Clarizen.
  • Account to Customer mapping: Accounts in your CRM are auto created or matched up with Clarizen customer records when a opportunity creates or updates a project in Clarizen.

  • Salesforce

  • Microsoft dynamics

    Ticketing systems

    Most organizations have a ticket management system before their processes mature enough for a project and portfolio management system like Clarizen. We understand that once you implement Clarizen, you will need to continue to manage the tickets in your chosen ticketing system. We have designed integrations to ticketing systems and a methodology that will allow you to do high level portfolio and project planning in Clarizen and continue to enable your technical resources to execute on tickets in your ticketing system.

      Use cases supported:
  • Sync each to a task for most granular 1:1 visibility in Clarizen. Progress and resources get synced automatically.
  • Sync each user story or epic (I.e. ticket group) to Clarizen workitem for visibility into a collection of tickets. Less granular but makes for a more efficient architecture. Allows your engineering resources to do their planning activities in ticketing system and your PMs can take it from there and fit the ticket collection into the larger overall initiative.

      • Supported Systems:
  • JIRA
  • Azure DevOps
  • Service Now
  • Gitlab
